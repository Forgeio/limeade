What's New

The Limeade rating system implements a Glicko-style rating algorithm that treats every play session as a match between the player and the level. Here's what has been added:

  • Player Skill Rating (SR): Tracks your overall skill level
  • Level Difficulty Rating (DR): Each level has its own difficulty score
  • Graded Outcomes: Not just win/loss - performance is measured on a scale from 0 to 1
  • Volatility Detection: Unfair or unpredictable levels are flagged
  • Multiple Leaderboards: Skill Rating, Seasonal, Blind Mode, and more
